Smart Inventory Reservation

Reserve items, confirm checkout, or cancel holds in real time.

1) Fetch Inventory

Enter a SKU to see product details and available quantity.

2) Reserve Inventory

Starts checkout by reserving inventory. Generates an idempotency key for safe retries.

3) Confirm Checkout

Confirms a reservation by its reservationId. Use the value returned from reserve.

4) Cancel Checkout

Cancels a reservation and restores inventory (or marks it expired if the hold is past due).

Backend endpoints used: GET /api/v1/inventory/:sku, POST /api/v1/inventory/reserve, POST /api/v1/checkout/confirm, POST /api/v1/checkout/cancel
